Inclusiveness Problem


In today's society, children with dyslexia are often disregarded as they go through their first steps of learning. The lack of resources for them and information for their parents to understand and help is an issue that needs more attention.

Sharing Our Solution


STEMlex is a website that aims to help dyslexic children in the process of learning by giving them a tool to find a font that helps them read their school texts easier.



Our Uniqueness


Considering the misconception that our society has about how hard it is going for a STEM career, we want to encourage children that use our webpage to get involved in STEM by introducing them to STEM books.

There's more to do!


STEMlex also includes tips, a flipcard game, and information for parents to help them understand how dyslexia may affect their child. Click on the different pages to discover them!
