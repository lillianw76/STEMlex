Use audiobooks: listen and read


Use audiobooks instead of reading alone when it becomes hard. You can listen the audiobook while you read it: being guided by the audio will help you to improve your reading!

Use highlighter: highlight those letters that you confuse


Relating letters or words you usually mix-up with specific colors will help you going though your text. Make sure to use always the same colors!
